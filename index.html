LAMPARA PROJECT

Monitoring your School is easier than ever!

The pandemic has posed significant challenges to the education sector, and the Lampara Project aims to address this by offering a suite of systems focused on simplicity and cost-efficiency. Our goal is to help Filipino learners adapt to the evolving learning environment.
We are committed to supporting schools as they transition to blended learning, providing effective implementation strategies that benefit both students and educational institutions. Our cloud-based, integrated systems offer real-time monitoring and seamless report generation, empowering schools to stay on track in these changing times.

SCHOOL MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Lampara School Management System is an advanced information management solution designed for educational institutions to efficiently manage all student data. It offers features such as student registration, grade documentation, and performance analysis, along with other evaluation tools. Additionally, the system supports curriculum planning, attendance tracking, and the management of all student-related activities within the school, streamlining administrative tasks and improving overall efficiency.

Lampara School Management System varies in size, scope, and capabilities, ranging from packages designed for smaller organizations that focus on student records, to enterprise-wide solutions that address most aspects of operations for large institutions and their online schools with significant local responsibilities. Many systems can be customized by adding additional modules, allowing institutions to tailor the system to their specific needs and scale functionality as required.

LEARNING MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

The Lampara Learning Management System is designed to enable partner schools to deliver educational modules in a cost-efficient manner. While the use of LMS gained momentum during the pandemic, we anticipate that these technologies will remain essential even in the post-pandemic era. To ensure long-term relevance, we have designed our LMS to seamlessly support both hybrid face-to-face and remote learning environments through the implementation of Flipped Learning adapting to the evolving needs of education.

ACCOUNTING MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Monitoring finances is crucial for the business operations of school institutions. In collaboration with our partners, we’ve developed an Accounting Management System integrated with our School Management System. Our Accounting Management System connects to online servers to ensure all transactions are securely backed up in the cloud, while also allowing data to be stored locally on devices for offline access. This means the system remains fully functional, whether or not there is an internet connection. Key features include student payment collections, ledger management, and comprehensive reporting, all accessible through the system for efficient financial tracking.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Lampara?

    Lampara is an integrated School Management System and Learning Management System, designed to streamline data processing and enhance efficiency in educational institutions.

  • Why "Lampara"?

    "Lampara" is a Filipino term that translates to "lamp" in English. In the wake of the pandemic, considered one of the darkest times for the education sector, the Lampara System is designed to light the way forward, offering support to our partner schools and institutions.

  • Who is Pro-Solutions Technology, Co.?

    Pro-Solutions Technology, Co. is the creator and developer of the Lampara System. Our team’s mission is to support educational institutions in their digital transformation journey, providing innovative solutions to enhance their operations.

  • How can we access our School Management System (SMS)?

    The website address for the School Management System (SMS) will be provided by Pro-Solutions Technology, Co. to your school. Please contact your school administrator for the SMS website link.

  • How can we access our Learning Management System (LMS)?

    The Learning Management System (LMS) is accessible via web application. Simply enter https://lmsv4.lamparaschool.ph in your browser (Chrome, Edge, Opera, Safari, etc.) to get started.

  • How can I get my username and password for Lampara?

    Your username and password are managed by the school administrator. Please contact your school for your personal login credentials. Note that your school has the ability to manage your account, including changing your password and updating your personal information.

  • What gadgets do we need in order to access Lampara?

    At Pro-Solutions, our goal is to make the Lampara system accessible to as many users as possible. That’s why we developed it as a web application. Any device with internet access and a web browser can connect to the Lampara system, including desktop PCs, laptops, tablets, and mobile phones.

  • Is Lampara available offline?

    Lampara is a web application, so an internet connection is required to access the system. However, any files uploaded by the teacher as lesson attachments can be downloaded. Once downloaded, these files will be available on your device, even without an internet connection.

  • Can I access Lampara with Mobile Data?

    Yes, you can access Lampara using mobile data, as long as you have a stable internet connection.

  • Will Lampara eat-up a lot of mobile internet data?

    The Lampara development team uses advanced frameworks with large support communities to optimize data processing. Thanks to continuous improvements in these frameworks, Lampara is designed to minimize internet data usage, ensuring efficient performance with the least possible data consumption.

  • Does Lampara LMS has a video conferencing service?

    Currently, Lampara LMS does not include a built-in video conferencing service. However, users are free to choose from various video conferencing platforms, and we recommend some of the free apps available online. Our development team is actively working on creating an integrated video conferencing service, so stay tuned for future updates!
