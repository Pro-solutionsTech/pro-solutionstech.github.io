LAMPARA PROJECT

Monitoring your School is easier than ever!

We consider this pandemic to be a dark time for the education sector. The Lampara Project is a set of systems focusing on simplicity and cost-efficiency to help the Filipino Learners adapt to the changing learning environment.

Our objective is to help schools continue their journey towards blended learning, through a sound implementation that is beneficial to the students as well as the school institution. We offer cloud-based integrated systems with real-time monitoring and report generation.

SCHOOL MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Lampara School Management System is an information management system for educational institutions to manage all student's data. It provides abilities such as registration of students in classes, documenting of grades and analytical marks of each student and other evaluation elements. In addition, an SMS is used for planning students’ curricula, recording their attendance and managing all student-related needs within a school.

Lampara School Management System vary in size, scope, and abilities, from packages implemented in relatively small organizations to cover student records only, to enterprise-wide solutions, which aim to cover most aspects of the operation of large organizations and their online schools with significant local responsibility. Many systems can be staggered at different levels of functionality, by purchasing additional "modules" and usually formed by their home institutions to meet local needs.

LEARNING MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

The Lampara Learning Management System is designed to allow partner-school to continue delivery of modules thru a cost-efficient way. Even though the utilization of LMS is reinforced by the pandemic, our team foresees that the use of these technologies will continue even post-pandemic. ACCOUNTING MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

It is important for the business-side of school-institutions to be able to monitor finances. With the help of our partners, we developed a system that is integrated with our School Management System. Our Accounting Management System connects with our online servers to ensure that all transactions are backed-up in the cloud, while being able to save data as local device backup. This means that our Accounting Management System works with or without the presence of the internet. Student Payment Collections, Ledgering, and Reports are available in the system.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Lampara?

    Lampara is a School Management System and a Learning Management System, combined for efficiency of data processing.

  • Why "Lampara"?

    Lampara is a Filipino term that translates to “lamp” in English. This pandemic is considered to be one of the darkest times of the education section. The Lampara System is intended to help light the way, in assistance with our partner school institutions.

  • Who is Pro-Solutions Technology, Co.?

    Pro-Solutions Technology, Co. is the creator and developer of the Lampara System. The objective of the team is to help school institutions with their digital transformation.

  • How can we access our School Management System (SMS)?

    The school management system website address is given by Pro-Solutions Technology, Co. to your school. Kindly refer to the school administrator for your SMS Website

  • How can we access our Learning Management System (LMS)?

    The learning management system is available as a web application by typing https://lms.lamparaschool.ph in your browser (Chrome, Edge, Opera, Safari, etc.).

  • How can I get my username and password for Lampara?

    The username and password is handled by the school administrator. Kindly refer to your school for your personal username and password. Please note that your school has the function to manage your accounts including changing your password, adjusting personal information, etc.

  • What gadgets do we need in order to access Lampara?

    Pro-Solutions wants majority of the users to be able to access the system, thus we created it as a Web Application initially. With this, any device that has access to the internet thru a browser can access the Lampara Web Application Link. This includes Desktop PCs, Laptops, Tablets, and Mobile Phones.

  • Is Lampara available offline?

    Lampara is a web application, therefore, internet connection is required in order to access the system. However, all files uploaded by the teacher as Lesson Attachments are downloadable. Once downloaded, the files are already available in your device even without the presence of the internet.

  • Can I access Lampara with Mobile Data?

    Yes. You can access Lampara using Mobile Data.

  • Will Lampara eat-up a lot of mobile internet data?

    The Lampara development team uses update development frameworks with the largest support community. With the advancements and improvements of such frameworks, data processing is optimized. Lampara is designed to function at the least possible internet data requirement.

  • Does Lampara LMS has a video conferencing service?

    Unfortunately, not yet. As of now, users are free to choose the video conferencing service they want to use. We are even recommending the free apps available on the internet. However, our development team is already designing and developing our own video conferencing service. Watch out for future updates!
